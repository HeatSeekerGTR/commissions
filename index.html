HeatSeekerGT

Your friendly neighborhood illustrator!

Hello there, visitor! My name is Edvins (known under alias HeatSeekerGT). I'm a 22 year old hobbyist illustrator specializing in various character visual renditions. I'm mostly self-taught with a background in creative clubs for youth, company logo design and freelance character illustration. I have been drawing since the age of 16 and started with simple armor designs. After discovering the culture of anime and its derivatives, I grew a liking to the style that can be expressed in various ways with and without detail. With this portfolio I aim to give you an insight of my illustrative profiency, and to assure you that your request will be treated with utmost importance and respect. Thank you for visiting and don't hesitate to contact with any questions!


Terms of Service

This section contains important information regarding ordering and processing of said order.

    I want to order an illustration!
  1. Sure! Contact me with your request details and/or questions! (Contact links at the bottom of the page. An answer will come within 24h depending on schedule.)
  2. I'll create a rough sketch of your request. (1-2 days. No payment required.)
  3. You may request any modifications, details or changes to your liking.
  4. If the sketch satisfies you, I'll send you a PayPal invoice with payment information.
  5. Once payment is through, I am off to make your wish a reality!
    Although I try to be flexible with illustration subjects and themes, I respectfully ask to consider the following due to my limited field of expertise:
  1. I have no experience in drawing robots, aliens, animals. (Animal details like tails, ears or human-like androids are fine.)
  2. I am unable to draw certain fetishes. (Such as age regression, inflation, bimbo, etc.)
  3. I am unable to create illustrations involving furry characters.
  4. I am unable to draw explicit adult content, but suggestive themes can be arranged. I respectfully decline to use characters that appear as minors in suggestive illustrations.
  5. Explicitly detailed backgrounds are not my level of expertise, but they can be arranged with an additional cost. Please inquire.


    Usage rights and service terms
  1. For non-commercial commissions, I grant you (the client) modification rights. You have the perpetual and worldwide right and permission to use, distribute, reproduce, adapt, modify and display the product so long as no profit is made, and proper credit is given. Credit must include at least my artist name (HeatSeekerGT - capitals not enforced) and one social media page, and/or my website. If the product is distributed in print form, please credit my website: https://heatseekergt.github.io/commissions/
  2. I (illustrator) and you (client) will not disclose any sensitive personal information of the other party. This includes but is not limited to: physical addresses, email addresses or payment information.
  3. Refunds of commissions are on a case-by-case basis. I will provide refunds if I am unable to finish the work, if you cancel the commission, or if I cancel the commission.
    The amount refunded will reflect on the amount of finish the artwork has. 70% of the commission price will be refunded if cancelled during the linework phase, 50% of the commission price will be refunded if cancelled during the flat color phase. Please note, after the flat color phase, the commission may not be cancelled unless I do so, or if I am unable to complete your commission.
  4. Please understand that due to the current tense situation in the world with respect to COVID-19, there may or may not be slight delays in communication or illustration delivery. Thank you for your understanding!

    5. If you agree to these terms and are ready to order an illustration, please proceed with ordering.
