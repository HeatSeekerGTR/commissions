HeatSeekerGTR

Your friendly neighborhood artist!

Hi there, visitor! My name is Peter Stark. I'm a hobbyist beginner artist specializing in drawing cartoon/anime-ish characters and such. Like most artists in this category, I'm self-taught and currently living off this as a generic, borrowed style that allows me to express myself. I'm still learning and grasping the larger art world out there and slowly getting the hang of it, but my skills are not in the state to present anything else yet.

Price sheet

Finally we arrive at the "goodies" section! Pricing is flat for everyone's convenience. Price changes do not apply to requests/commissions already in sketch phase.

20 $

Digital portrait

Bust to head is the maximum height. A portrait aimed to appreciate your character's unique and exclusive personality. Fully soft-shaded to the smallest detail.

Work takes 2-3 days. Basic background optional.

20 $

Lineart

Weighted lines will accentuate your character's level of detail and completely visualize your ideas. Our only limit is imagination.

Work takes 2-4 days. Colored eyes are optional and free of charge.

30 $

Cell shading

Colors are an extremely valuable asset that adds a completely new level of charm to your character. Add a little bit of shaded depth and see it come to life!

Work takes 3-7 days. Basic background optional.

40 $

Full soft shading

Colors are not enough. You want everything. I shall devote myself to my best ability and work at peak-performance to make your character look like no other. No compromises. No exceptions.

Work takes ~1 week. In extreme cases a few days over. Reason being my technique - I let the work go for a day or two and then come back to it with a fresh mind to spot any errors as I want only the best for my client.
